Our Story

Brewing excellence since 2005

Our Mission

To create a welcoming space where everyone can experience the perfect cup of coffee, made with passion and served with care. We strive to source the finest beans, support sustainable farming practices, and provide exceptional service to our community.

Our Vision

To be the most loved coffee brand that brings people together, inspires creativity, and makes a positive impact in every community we serve. We envision a world where great coffee is accessible to everyone, and every cup tells a story.

How It All Began

MR.COFFEE was founded in 2010 by John Smith, a passionate coffee enthusiast with a dream to create a space where people could enjoy exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. What started as a small coffee shop has grown into a beloved community hub, serving thousands of customers daily.

Our journey has been marked by a commitment to quality, sustainability, and community. We source our beans directly from farmers who share our values, ensuring fair trade practices and environmental responsibility.

Our Journey

2005
MR.COFFEE began as a small coffee cart in downtown, serving our first cup of coffee to the community.
2008
Opened our first permanent location, introducing our signature blend and expanding our menu.
2012
Launched our sustainable coffee sourcing program, partnering directly with farmers in South America.
2015
Expanded to multiple locations and introduced our mobile app for convenient ordering.
2020
Launched our rewards program and became the city's most popular coffee destination.
2023
Continuing to grow while staying true to our roots and commitment to quality coffee.
